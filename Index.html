Restaurant Revamp: Match the Change

Welcome to the game! In this exercise, you will be presented with scenario cards that describe change initiatives in a local restaurant. Your task is to drag and drop each scenario card into the correct change management category.

For every correct placement, you will earn 10 points (the card will turn green), and for every incorrect attempt, 5 points will be deducted (the card will briefly flash red). Read each scenario carefully and match it to the appropriate topic below:

  1. Strategic Approaches to Change
  2. Transition State in Change
  3. Choosing Organizational Structures
  4. Diversity in Change Management
  5. Motivation and Resistance
  6. Managing Talent
  7. Learning for Change (Online Learning)
  8. HR and Change
  9. Leadership in Change
  10. Teams and Team Dynamics
  11. Employee Health and Wellbeing
